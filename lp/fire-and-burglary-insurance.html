Fire & Burglary Insurance

Fire & Burglary Insurance provides coverage for physical damage to your property. This damage can occur in a variety of situations like:

1- Fire
2- Lightning
3- Explosion/implosion
4- Aircraft Damage
5- Riot, Strike and Malicious Damage
6- Storm, Cyclone, Typhoon, Tempest, Hurricane, Tornado, Flood and Inundation
7- Impact Damage
8- Subsidence and Landslide including Rock slide
9- Bursting and/or overflowing of Water Tanks, Apparatus and Pipes 10- Missile Testign operations
11- Leakage from Automatic Sprinkler Installations
12- Bush Fire

This insurance is designed to meet the needs of businesses of various sizes, with three coverage options available:
  1. Sookshma Udyam Suraksha - covers assets up to Rs. 5 crore
  2. Laghu Udyam Suraksha - covers assets between Rs. 5 to 50 crore
  3. Standard Fire Special Perils (SFSP) - covers assets exceeding Rs. 50 crore

Fire accidents are a common cause of accidents in India's residential and commercial sectors. In 2021 alone, there were 1.6 million fire incidents recorded in the country.

To ensure the growth and stability of your company, it is crucial to secure your assets. Fire & Burglary Insurance provides protection for your property and its contents against various events, including fire, theft, natural disasters, riots, and terrorism.

This insurance offers customized protection options that cater to your business needs. You can choose from specialized coverage choices like Sookshma Udyam Suraksha, Laghu Udyam Suraksha, and Standard Fire Special Peril (SFSP).

Fire and burglary insurance protects your business and its assets, shielding them from potential losses caused by fires or thefts.  It covers a range of incidents related to fire and break-ins.

  • Fire-related loss
  • Damage due to aircraft
  • Natural Calamity
  • Explosion/Implosion
  • Damage to personal property
  • Loss to an adjacent property
  • Violent and forceful entry
  • Armed robbery
  • Bush Fire
  • Business Interruption

Companies that own or rent assets in the form of office premises, stock, machinery, cash, or other forms need comprehensive fire and burglary insurance policies.

In addition to covering the office, retail, or factory premises, this policy also protects the contents inside the premises including electronic equipment like laptops, computers, and phones.

Fire & burglary insurance offers valuable coverage for businesses of all sizes and industries.

A few common businesses that purchase fire insurance are:

  • Manufacturing and Industrial Units: Safeguard your operations, vehicles, and equipment with fire insurance.
  • Banks and Other Institutions: Ensure the protection of your financial assets and operations with fire insurance.
  • Hotel Owners and Service Providers: Guard against the financial consequences of fire damage to your hospitality or service-based business.
  • Medical Clinics: Protect your medical facility, equipment, and patient records from fire damage.
  • Shop-keepers and Godown Owners: Protect your retail or storage space from the potential financial impact of a fire.
